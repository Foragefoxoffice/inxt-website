## Simple, Smart Invoicing

Our **intelligent invoicing system** streamlines your billing process with professional invoice templates that can be customized with your branding, automated invoice generation based on recurring services, and intelligent payment reminders to reduce late payments. Create, send, and manage invoices effortlessly while keeping every transaction organized and on time. Track invoice status in real-time, from draft to sent, viewed, and paid, with automatic updates and notifications.

## What's included

Built for businesses that want to simplify invoicing and get paid faster, our system automates the entire billing workflow.

- **Professional invoice templates** – Customize with your branding and company details.
- **Automated invoice generation** – Create invoices automatically for recurring services.
- **Intelligent payment reminders** – Reduce late payments with smart notification systems.
- **Real-time status tracking** – Monitor invoices from draft to paid.
- **Multi-currency support** – Invoice clients in their preferred currency.
